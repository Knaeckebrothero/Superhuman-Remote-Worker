# Selected Requirements for FINIUS GmbH

The following requirements (REQ-001 – REQ-068) have been identified as relevant to FINIUS GmbH's business objects and compliance obligations. Each entry includes the requirement text, source reference (page and section), and the associated Business Objects (BO) that the requirement impacts.

| ID | Requirement | Source | Business Objects |
|----|-------------|--------|-------------------|

| REQ-002 | Books and the required records may be kept on data carriers if the method complies with the GoB according to § 146 Abs. 5 AO. | Page 5, Section 1.5 | All electronic records |
| REQ-003 | All business transactions must be recorded individually, completely, correctly, timely, orderly, and immutable as required by §§ 146 AO and § 239 HGB. | Page 9, Section 3.2 | Booking, Invoice, Payment, Customer |
| REQ-004 | The tax authority must be able to trace each transaction from the source document through the journal to the balance sheet (progressive and retrograde auditability). | Page 10, Section 3.1 | Booking, Invoice, Payment |
| REQ-005 | Electronic books and records must be accessible for the duration of the retention period and must support verification of the processing procedures. | Page 9, Section 3.1‑3.2 | Invoice, Payment |
| REQ-006 | The taxpayer is solely responsible for the correctness of electronic books and records, even if processing is outsourced. | Page 8, Section 2 | All financial records |
| REQ-007 | Data processing systems (main, sub, and ancillary) used for bookkeeping must be documented and capable of supporting the requirements of §§ 145‑147 AO. | Page 8, Section 1.11 | IT System (supporting all BO) |
| REQ-008 | All records, whether on paper or electronic, that are necessary for understanding and verifying tax‑relevant information must be retained. | Page 5, Section 1.3 | All BO |

| REQ-018 | All business transactions must be recorded in chronological order with materially correct and recognizable content. | Page 21, Section 5.1 | Booking |

| REQ-025 | Incorrect bookings may be corrected by cancellations or new bookings in a traceable manner. | Page 24, Section 5.3 | Booking |

| REQ-040 | Converting PDF/A‑3 files to image formats (TIFF, JPEG, etc.) is not allowed because it destroys embedded XML data and full‑text information. | Page 31, Section 9.1 | All BO |
| REQ-041 | Converting electronic ground‑book records to PDF format is permissible only if the conversion does not restrict machine‑readability. | Page 31, Section 9.1 | All BO |
| REQ-042 | Converting records to an in‑house format is allowed provided that machine‑readability is preserved and no content is altered. | Page 31, Section 9.1 | All BO |

| REQ-048 | Encrypted electronic documents must be available in decrypted form within the IT system to ensure accessibility. | Page 34, Section 9.3 | All BO |
