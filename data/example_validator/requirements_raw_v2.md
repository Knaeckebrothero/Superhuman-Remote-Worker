# Raw Requirements (Phase 2)

## Batch 1‑10 Extracted Requirements

TEMP-001 | Non‑tax bookkeeping and recording obligations that are relevant for taxation must also be fulfilled for tax law purposes. | Page 3, Section 1.1 | [5]
TEMP-002 | Books and the required records may be kept on data carriers if the method complies with the GoB according to § 146 Abs. 5 AO. | Page 5, Section 1.5 | [6]
TEMP-003 | All business transactions must be recorded individually, completely, correctly, timely, orderly, and immutable as required by §§ 146 AO and § 239 HGB. | Page 9, Section 3.2 | [6]
TEMP-004 | The tax authority must be able to trace each transaction from the source document through the journal to the balance sheet (progressive and retrograde auditability). | Page 10, Section 3.1 | [6]
TEMP-005 | Electronic books and records must be accessible for the duration of the retention period and must support verification of the processing procedures. | Page 9, Section 3.1‑3.2 | [6]
TEMP-006 | The taxpayer is solely responsible for the correctness of electronic books and records, even if processing is outsourced. | Page 8, Section 2 | [6]
TEMP-007 | Data processing systems (main, sub, and ancillary) used for bookkeeping must be documented and capable of supporting the requirements of §§ 145‑147 AO. | Page 8, Section 1.11 | [6]
TEMP-008 | All records, whether on paper or electronic, that are necessary for understanding and verifying tax‑relevant information must be retained. | Page 5, Section 1.3 | [6]
TEMP-009 | The principle of “Nachvollziehbarkeit und Nachprüfbarkeit” (traceability and auditability) must be maintained throughout the retention period. | Page 10, Section 3.1 | [6]
TEMP-010 | The taxpayer must ensure that the technical standards (e.g., archival media, cryptographic methods) are appropriate for the longevity of the records. | Page 6, Section 1.6 | [6]

## Batch 11‑20 Extracted Requirements

TEMP-011 | Individual recording of each purchase transaction is prohibited; duplicate recordings are not allowed. | Page 11 | [7]
TEMP-012 | The individual recording obligation applies also to electronic recording systems, even when the system is certified. | Page 11 | [7]
TEMP-013 | Exception to individual recording is permitted only when it is technically, economically, and practically impossible. | Page 11 | [7]
TEMP-014 | Cash receipts and cash payments must be recorded daily according to § 146 Abs. 1 Satz 2 AO. | Page 11 | [7]
TEMP-015 | Electronic books and records must ensure immutability as required by § 146 Abs. 4 AO and § 239 Abs. 3 HGB. | Page 12 | [8]
